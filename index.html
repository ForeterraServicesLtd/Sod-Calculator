About Foreterra Services

Trusted property services across the Peace Region

Foreterra Services is a Grande Prairie-based property services company built by people who know the land. We handle everything from sod installation and lot sweeping to herbicide application and full-season lawn maintenance. Whether it's a residential yard or a commercial property, we bring the right crew, the right equipment, and honest pricing every time.

Service Area

Grande Prairie, Clairmont, Sexsmith, Beaverlodge, Wembley, and surrounding Peace Region communities.

What Sets Us Apart

Transparent pricing calculators, licensed applicators, proper equipment for every job, and a crew that shows up on time.

For Everyone

Homeowners can post jobs and get bids. Contractors can find work and price it right. One platform for the whole community.
