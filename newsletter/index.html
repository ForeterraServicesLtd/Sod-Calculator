April Landscape Tips

April To-Do List for Your Yard

  • 🌾
    Aerate and overseed — April is the ideal time to aerate compacted soil and overseed thin or bare patches before the growing season fully kicks in.
  • 💧
    Check irrigation systems — Turn on your irrigation and inspect for broken heads, leaks, or coverage gaps after the winter months.
  • ✂️
    Prune spring shrubs — After flowering, trim your lilacs and other spring bloomers to keep them healthy and shapely heading into summer.
  • 🌿
    Feed your soil — Apply a slow-release fertilizer this month to give your lawn the nutrients it needs for peak growing season.
